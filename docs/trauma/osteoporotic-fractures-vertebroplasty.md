## Evidence base

!!! question "Controversy: does vertebroplasty actually work?"
    Five major blinded trials give genuinely conflicting answers depending on fracture chronicity and comparator.

    | Study | Design | Finding |
    |---|---|---|
    | VAPOUR, Clark W et al., *Lancet* 2016 | Double-blind, placebo-controlled, acute (<6 week) fractures | Positive for vertebroplasty. |
    | VERTOS IV, Firanescu C et al., *BMJ* 2018 | Randomised, sham-controlled, acute fractures | Negative, no benefit over sham. |
    | VERTOS V, *Radiology* 2023 | Active-control (not sham), chronic (>3 month) fractures | Vertebroplasty superior to anaesthetic infiltration at 12 months. |
