# Infection

Pyogenic and granulomatous infection of the spine.

- [Pyogenic Spinal Infection](pyogenic-infection.md), including discitis and epidural abscess
- [Spinal Tuberculosis](spinal-tuberculosis.md)

Infection is a named critical condition in the T&O curriculum (Appendix 3), and is one of the differentials that must be actively excluded whenever a patient presents with new back pain and systemic upset, worth cross-referencing from the relevant degenerative pages once those carry a red-flags section.
