## Evidence base

| Study | Key finding |
|---|---|
| Weinstein SL et al., BrAIST, *NEJM* 2013 | Landmark RCT: bracing significantly reduced progression to surgical threshold vs observation. Stopped early on ethical grounds. Strong dose-response with wear time (>12.9h/day gave 90-93% success). |
| Vertebral body tethering series (cited in recent AIS/AdIS review literature) | One series reported 95% success avoiding fusion at 5 years. Promising but a much younger, smaller evidence base than bracing's. |
