# Inflammatory

Rheumatological and inflammatory conditions with spinal manifestations, an area where the spine can be affected quite differently to peripheral joints and where the trauma risk is easy to underestimate.

- [Rheumatoid Cervical Spine](rheumatoid-cervical-spine.md), atlantoaxial and subaxial instability
- [Ankylosing Spondylitis](ankylosing-spondylitis.md), including the fracture risk in an ossified spine

Both conditions are named in the T&O curriculum's critical conditions list under "complications of inflammatory spinal conditions", worth stating plainly on both pages once written, as it's an easy critical condition to forget sits here rather than under Trauma.
