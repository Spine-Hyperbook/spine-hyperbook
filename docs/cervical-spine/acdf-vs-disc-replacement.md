## Evidence base

| Study | Key finding |
|---|---|
| Systematic review with meta-analysis, 14 RCTs, 3160 patients, *Bone Joint J* 2018 | TDR superior to ACDF at 2 years and 4-7 years on NDI, SF-36, dysphagia, satisfaction; less adjacent segment disease. |
| Ten-year outcomes of cervical disc arthroplasty vs ACDF, systematic review with meta-analysis, 2024 | Confirms durability of TDR's advantage to a full decade of follow-up. |
| Retrospective single-surgeon comparative study, mean 5.4 years, 2022/2023 | No significant difference in complication or reoperation rate in real-world practice, a counterweight to the more favourable RCT meta-analyses. |
