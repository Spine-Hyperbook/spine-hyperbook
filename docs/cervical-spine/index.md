# Cervical Spine

Degenerative, deformity, and instability conditions affecting C1-C7. Cervical trauma is covered separately under [Trauma](../trauma/index.md), and inflammatory conditions affecting the cervical spine (rheumatoid, ankylosing spondylitis) sit under [Inflammatory](../inflammatory/index.md).

- [Cervical Radiculopathy](cervical-radiculopathy.md), nerve root compression, usually self-limiting
- [Cervical Myelopathy](cervical-myelopathy.md), spinal cord compression, tends to progress without treatment
- [ACDF vs Cervical Disc Replacement](acdf-vs-disc-replacement.md), a direct comparison for when either is a reasonable option
- [Craniocervical Junction & Atlantoaxial Instability](craniocervical-junction.md)
