# Thoracic Spine

The smallest section on the site by volume, and genuinely so, thoracic pathology is less common than cervical or lumbar disease, partly because the rib cage adds substantial stability that the rest of the spine doesn't have.

- [Thoracic Disc Herniation](thoracic-disc-herniation.md)

Thoracic trauma and thoracic deformity are covered under [Trauma](../trauma/index.md) and [Deformity](../deformity/index.md) respectively rather than here, this section is specifically for thoracic degenerative disease.
